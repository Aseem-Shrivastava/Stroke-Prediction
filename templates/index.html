Welcome to Stroke Predictor

This application allows you to predict the likelihood of a stroke based on individual health data and lifestyle choices. By providing information such as age, gender, BMI, glucose levels, and other health indicators, our machine learning model will analyze the data and provide an accurate prediction of stroke risk.

This tool is intended to provide quick insights and help you make informed decisions about your health. Please note that this is not a substitute for medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for any health concerns.
